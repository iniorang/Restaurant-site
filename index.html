The Authentic Indonesia’s Heritage Cuisine with Organic and Natural Ingredients

Welcome to your life-changing journey

Experience the authentic taste of Indonesia's cultural heritage with Warung Hati's cuisine. Our menu is crafted with indigenous herbs, spices, and natural ingredients that have been passed down from our ancestors. We believe that this wisdom is the key to achieving the balance between humans and nature, and is also the secret to prime health and longevity.

Nasi Timbel

A whole food meal from Sundanese consisting of Sayur Asem, sambal, ungkep kampong chicken, and organic rice wrapped with banana leaves.

Gado-Gado

A mix of steamed and raw vegetables, fried tofu, and boiled egg, with creamy sweet-savory cashew dressing. Javanese Island's most popular salad! Every city in Java has its style of this salad but with one similarity: delicious!

Gudeg Jogja

Unlock Java's heart with sweetly stewed young jackfruit. A fragrant blend of coconut milk, palm sugar, and spices weaves a captivating tale of flavors.

Jajanan Pasar

Delight in Jajan Pasar's treasures. Assorted traditional sweets showcase Indonesia's culinary heritage, embodying flavors that enchant generations.

Es Cendol

Layers of joy—pandan rice boba, coconut milk, and palm sugar syrup. An icy indulgence that's sweet, creamy, and utterly refreshing.
